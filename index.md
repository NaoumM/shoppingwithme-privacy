Privacy Policy — Shopping with me
Last updated: March 2026
Shopping with me does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
What we collect
Nothing. The app does not require an account, does not track your location, and does not use any analytics or advertising tools.
Your shopping lists
All your lists and items are stored locally on your device. If you choose to share a list, the list data is synced via Firebase solely to enable real-time collaboration with people you invite. This data is not used for any other purpose.
Third-party services
The app uses Google Firebase Realtime Database only when you explicitly enable list sharing. No personal information is sent to Firebase — only the list content you choose to share.
